
#Title:
Lexical decision task with coloured Italian pseudowords

#Reference (poster presentation):
Šaban, I., Marson, F., Loca, G., Marelli, M. (November 2024). Colorful, but never meaningless: Morphological decomposition and semantic processing of complex pseudowords. Psychonomics; New York, USA

#Data source:
https://osf.io/sd63u/overview

#Description:
- Instruction: Is the stimulus on the screen an Italian word?
- 1 out of 2 response options: "yes" and "no"
- Experiment 1: 9882 datapoints from 27 participants, Experiment 2: 5670 datapoints from 30 participants
